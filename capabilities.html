Research Capabilities

End-to-end genomic analysis for infectious pathogens.

Our work covers sequencing, QC, interpretation, and reproducible reporting. We support projects in clinical and environmental microbiology through scalable, method-aware analysis pipelines.

Core Domains

  • Viral, bacterial, and fungal genomics
  • Shotgun metagenomics and 16S profiling
  • WGS assembly, annotation, and comparative genomics
  • Phylogenetics and AMR marker profiling
  • Pipeline engineering and reproducibility documentation

Whole Genome Sequencing

Reference-guided and de novo workflows with quality filtering, assembly checks, and annotation.

Metagenomic Profiling

Taxonomic and functional characterization of mixed microbial communities from complex samples.

16S rRNA Analysis

Targeted microbial community studies with transparent methods and reproducible outputs.

Phylogenetics

Evolutionary and transmission-oriented analyses for outbreak and surveillance investigations.

AMR Profiling

Genomic detection and reporting of antimicrobial resistance determinants for research workflows.

Pipeline Development

Automated, versioned computational pipelines suitable for collaborative and grant-funded studies.

Data Types

Sample contexts we handle

  • Clinically relevant infectious disease specimens
  • Environmental microbial monitoring samples
  • High-complexity mixed microbial samples
  • Longitudinal and cross-sectional cohort data

Deliverables

Standard outputs for collaborators

  • Quality metrics and reproducibility notes
  • Taxonomic and genomic summary reports
  • Variant and comparative genomic interpretations
  • Project-ready figures, tables, and methods text

Partnership Fit

Ideal collaborations

We are interested in co-authored research, grant consortia, and studies requiring coordinated clinical and computational expertise in infectious disease genomics.
