Infectious Disease Genomics

We integrate laboratory science, genomics, and bioinformatics for infectious disease surveillance and response.

Built over years of hands-on experience, our work spans viral, bacterial, and fungal genomics, including metagenomics, 16S profiling, and WGS for clinically and environmentally relevant samples.
