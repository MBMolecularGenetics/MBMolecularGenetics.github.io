Research-Focused Collaborative Hub

We bridge clinical microbiology, genomics, and bioinformatics for infectious disease research.

This initiative connects real-world infectious disease contexts with advanced sequencing and computation. We operate across viral, bacterial, and fungal genomics with integrated wet-lab and in silico workflows for clinically and environmentally relevant samples.

Positioning

Specialized initiative, not a loose network

We position ourselves as a unified research initiative focused on pathogen genomics, metagenomics, and computational microbiology. Our objective is to co-develop high-impact studies with strong methodological and translational relevance.

Partnership Model

Designed for collaborative projects and grant work

We support collaborations through defined analysis packages, co-authorship pathways, and multi-center study design. Our website and outputs are structured for external partners who need rigorous and transparent genomic workflows.
